# Community Support

We are here to help you get the most out of kubara and connect with other members of the community.  
Whether you have questions, need troubleshooting assistance, or just want to share ideas - join us!

---

## 💬 kubara Community Channel
Join our Google Chat space to ask questions, discuss ideas, and stay up to date with the latest news.  
[**Kubara Community Channel**](https://mail.google.com/chat/u/0/#chat/space/AAQAKAHvYwo)

## 📅 kubara Office Hours
We host weekly office hours where you can talk directly with the maintainers and other community members.

- **When:** Every Thursday, 10:00-11:00 (German time, CET/CEST)
- **Where:** [Google Meet - kubara Office Hours](https://meet.google.com/udh-ijrp-jqb)

Bring your questions, feedback, or anything you would like to discuss!

---

## 🙌 Be respectful
Our community follows the [Code of Conduct](../6_reference/code_of_conduct.md).
